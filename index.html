About Me

Welcome to my website! Here you'll find a little about me including my background and computer skills.

I'm a college student currently pursuing a Computer Science degree at the University of California, Santa Cruz. My computer skills are diverse; I primarily focus on native applications developed in languages such as C and Java, but I also possess a background in web design, including the use of technologies such as HTML, Javascript, and CSS, as well as Content Management Systems such as Wordpress, Omeka, and Drupal.

I particularly enjoy the specialties of Linux Systems Administration & Forensic Data Analysis. The problems of cybercrime and the role of encryption in today's society relate closely to these specialties, which is why I learn as much about them as I can. My learning experiences in this field include an internship at the Center for Advanced Computing Research at the California Institute of Technology. My role, which was to assist in the day to day management and maintenance of large-scale computing resources used by the university for academic research helped create a foundation in Systems Administration for me to build off of, and has been invaluable in educating me about how the world's computer networks work together as a whole.

When I'm not inside working on my computer, I can be found in the great outdoors, usually hiking, camping, or backpacking. I've particularly enjoyed climbing the famed Half Dome, in Yosemite National Park, as well as hiking throughout the San Gabriel Mountains north of Pasadena, California. I am also a proud Eagle Scout.
